about us

BUGS + PROPHITT

Source Code Vintage is a small business created by Bugs and Prophitt. We are a couple who have recently relocated from Chicago to Rochester, NY. We at Source Code have spent our whole lives acquiring, curating, and creating one of kind pieces and can't wait to share them with like-minded individuals. We have unique items in many ranges of styles and pricing that you wont find anywhere else. Some items available online. Find us in person at one of our events, inquire below!
